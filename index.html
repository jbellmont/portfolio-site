About

Jack is a passionate front end developer, skilled in HTML5, CSS3 and Python. These skills are built from hundreds of hours of self-development: taking online bootcamps, courses and pouring over documentation.

The skills have been applied to real-world portfolio projects, as seen below in the projects section; these focus on clean and responsive design and intuitive user interfaces - with accessibility always in mind.

He currently work in digital advertising and marketing, having worked with international and national clients such as Bacardi, Lexus and the Co-op, for a number of years.

In his career he has developed soft skills in:

  • Communicating technical concepts with non-technical stakeholders
  • Analysing and presenting complex data analysis
  • Attention to detail - having managed millions of Euros advertising budgets
  • Leading and working in cross-functional teams
  • Ability to prioritize concurrent projects

He wishes to switch to a more technical path and become a Front End Developer, where he can apply his core foundational skills in a commercial setting.
